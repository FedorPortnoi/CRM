# Bitrix24

## Overview

Bitrix24 is our primary displacement target. It is the dominant CRM in Eastern Europe and ex-CIS and growing globally as a low-cost Salesforce alternative. Many of our target customers (especially [[Micro-Businesses]] and [[Small Businesses]]) have already tried Bitrix24 and failed to adopt it. This creates a receptive audience: people who know they need a CRM but have a bad CRM experience.

**Parent company:** Bitrix Inc. (founded 1998, private)
**HQ:** Alexandria, VA (US), Kaliningrad, Russia (development)
**Users:** 12M+ registered users across 200+ countries

## Strengths

- Extremely feature-rich: CRM, project management, HR, telephony, website builder, video calls, document management — all in one
- Free tier available (up to 5 users with basic CRM features) — large top-of-funnel
- Strong in post-Soviet markets and SMBs looking to avoid Salesforce costs
- Can genuinely replace multiple tools for organizations willing to invest in learning it
- Self-hosted option available for enterprises with data residency requirements

## Weaknesses

- **Complexity is the defining pain point:** Onboarding typically takes 1–3 months; most small businesses use only 20% of features
- **Mobile app is a crippled afterthought:** Critical features require desktop; mobile performance is poor (slow loading, heavy)
- **UI is overwhelming for non-technical users:** Steep learning curve; cluttered, dated interface
- **Feature bloat creates confusion:** Users get lost trying to find the CRM inside a platform that also has HR, website builder, and corporate social network
- **Customer support for lower tiers is minimal:** Community-only support on free tier; response times slow on basic paid tiers
- **Sales automation is complex to configure:** Requires technical knowledge to set up automation rules correctly

## Watch Out For

- Bitrix is actively improving its mobile app — releasing updates regularly
- Their free tier creates strong lock-in through data and learned behavior
- In some markets (Russia, Eastern Europe, CIS), Bitrix has brand recognition we cannot match quickly
