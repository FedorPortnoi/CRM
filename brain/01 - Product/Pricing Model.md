# Pricing Model

## Guiding Principles

- Priced for SMBs — every tier must feel fair to a small business owner paying from their own pocket
- Per-seat, no hidden limits — predictable and transparent vs. HubSpot's painful tier jumps
- 14-day full-featured trial — no credit card, no freemium bait-and-switch
- Annual discount drives retention and provides predictable revenue

## Tier Structure

### Starter — $14/user/month (annual) | $18/month (monthly)
**For:** [[Solo Entrepreneurs]] and early [[Micro-Businesses]]
- All 14 MVP features
- Up to 2,500 contacts
- 3 pipelines
- 100 SMS/month
- 5GB storage
- Email support

### Growth — $24/user/month (annual) | $30/month (monthly)
**For:** [[Micro-Businesses]] and [[Small Businesses]]
- Everything in Starter
- Unlimited contacts + pipelines
- 500 SMS/month
- 20 automation rules
- 25GB storage
- Priority support (email + chat)
- Google Calendar sync

### Business — $39/user/month (annual) | $49/month (monthly)
**For:** [[Small Businesses]] and [[Medium Businesses]]
- Everything in Growth
- 2,000 SMS/month
- Unlimited automation rules
- API access + webhooks (v2)
- 100GB storage
- Custom analytics reports
- SLA (99.9% uptime)
- Dedicated account manager (20+ seats)

## Comparison vs Competitors

| Tier | Our Price | HubSpot Equivalent | Salesforce Equivalent |
|------|-----------|-------------------|----------------------|
| Starter | $14/user | $20/user | $25/user |
| Growth | $24/user | $100/user | $75/user |
| Business | $39/user | Custom | $150/user |

At the [[Small Businesses]] segment (30 users): $720/month vs HubSpot's $3,000/month. That's the story.
